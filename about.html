Who We Are.

Founded by Joanne Lamayen, J's KEOPi COFFEE & TEA stands as a recently established enterprise situated on E. Jacinto Street in Brgy. Sto. Niño, Marikina City, Philippines, with a formal postal code of 1900. This small-scale company specializes in providing an array of reasonably priced coffee, tea, and snacks to its clientele. Commencing operations in approximately July of 2023, J's KEOPi COFFEE & TEA has swiftly become a noteworthy presence in the local market, offering a diverse selection of beverages and snacks to cater to the discerning tastes of its patrons.

With an emphasis on delighting the senses, our carefully curated selection of premium coffee and tea blends, complemented by delectable snacks, guarantees a truly delightful indulgence. Each product is meticulously crafted to create a symphony of flavors, ensuring that every visit to J's KEOPi COFFEE & TEA is a memorable and satisfying experience for our discerning patrons.

Inspired by Egg Drop (에그드랍), which is the name of the famous Korean sandwich.
